Design Challenge
How might we provide for the enjoyment of condiments with take-out and delivery meals without single use plastics?
Process:
Research & Solution
I worked with my 3 design team members, Guglielmo Fonda, Elisey Goland, and Sofia Wheeler, to find a circular solution to wasteful condiment packaging.

We had 1 week for the challenge. In first researching and observing user interaction with existing products, we came to the conclusion that take-out food packaging will most likely be discarded for convenience regardless of its potential for reuse. Thus, we searched for a sustainable solution that could realistically be adopted by users with little to no compromise of convenience.
